About Us

Serenity is a events management company in Magaliesberg, Kimberley, South Africa owned by Teresa Cloete that helps manage your big ideas and smallest concerns. We create environments that WOW your guests, surpass your expectations and create long lasting memories.

For more than 5 years, we've been working with clients to create exceptional experiences for birthdays, weddings, dinners etc. and we are continuously evolving and actively pursuing new trends and technological advances. Here at Serenity you can hire anything you need for your event such as tables, chairs, center pieces and many more or book us for the whole package and we will do everything for your event

It is our goal to leave you and your guests with an event that exceeds expectations and lasts in your memories long after the last toast.
